Greetings! I am Chaitanya, a final year B.Tech Engineering Physics student (2018-2022) at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad, India. I am an aspiring theoretical physicist fascinated by the Standard Model of Particle Physics and many of the open problems associated with it. The particular revelation that motivates me is the striking synergy between mathematics, supposedly "invented" by humans, and the laws of physics used to describe Nature. To explore this idea in detail, please feel free to read this non-technical lecture on "The Relation between Mathematics and Physics" by Paul Dirac in 1939. Driven by this idea, I plan to contribute, develop and innovate mathematical techniques to describe interactions of elementary particles, thriving to provide a complete view of fundamental physics.



I'm looking forward to pursuing graduate studies in particle physics. Feel free to read about my experience and interests. I would be happy to get in touch. Apart from research, I like to spend my pastime with video editing and cinematography for all kinds of films. And I am always excited for Night sky observation sessions, Robotics, and casual sports.
